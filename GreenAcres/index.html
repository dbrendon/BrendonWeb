Hard to believe that there were over 70 people who came out for an elementary school reunion - we're special?!?

A major thank you to Martha for being there from the beginning, for committing herself to being at the reunion even if it meant it might be only the two of us in attendance. Thank you for putting up the money for the school tour (contributions from alumni just covered the cost) and following through on the paperwork. Thanks for making phone calls, purchasing the decorations, organizing the quiz and the door prizes. Thanks for taking the photos. Thanks for helping out on the big day and afterwards. I wouldn't have done it without you.

Thank you Debbie U. for making so many calls and contacts, your support and the flowers.

Thank you Sharon and Colleen for getting the word out.

Thank you David W. and Mrs. Menicanin for tracking down people.

Thank you Jim F. for donating the Stoney Creek Furniture door prizes.

Thank you Mr. Janzen, Jackie and Matt for your enthusiasm, especially in the earlier stages, you kept me motivated.

As well, thank you goes to the Roxxbury Nite Club which was a great venue.

Most of all thank you to everyone, Class of '77 and teachers, your response made it definitely worth the effort.

Take care,
Brenda

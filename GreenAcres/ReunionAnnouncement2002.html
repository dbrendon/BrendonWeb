Green Acres
Class of 1977
25th Anniversary Reunion
Friday, June 21, 2002

The event is for graduates, former classmates and teachers of the class of 1977 (people who entered Kindergarten at Green Acres or RL Hyslop in 1968).

Main Event:

The Main Event is at 8:00 pm on Friday, June 21, 2002 at The Roxxbury Nightclub 840 Queenston Road (Battlefield Square). The Roxxbury has been kind enough to provide us a room, complimentary appetizers, and will waive the cover charge for registered participants. Please note a dress code will be in effect (minimum no hats, runners or blue jeans). FYI there is usually a live to air feed with Energy FM 95.3 on Friday nights.

Pre-Main Event:

Depending on interest there may be a Green Acres walk through at 7:00 pm. In order for this to occur we need donations to cover the insurance costs. Please let the person you contact know if you are interested. We hope to confirm the walk through by June 5, 2002.

We're trying our best to contact everyone. Please let us know the whereabouts of any other classmates or teachers.

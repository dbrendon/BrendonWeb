December 31st - under the stars in the White Desert

  • awesome topography
  • mountain sand dunes
  • Black Desert; sand scattered with black stone, volcanic shaped mountains
  • barreling over the desert floor in ancient 4 wheel drive vehicle, skidding, fish tailing
  • White Desert; odd shapes, valley of the giant mushrooms
  • vastness of the desert and the absence of any vegetation what so ever
  • road side rest stops in the middle of no where
  • camel left by the side of the road
  • accompanying jeep breaking down and subsequent failed attempts to repair, surprisingly an additional jeep arrived the next morning
  • flower rock valley
  • crystal mountain
  • meal cooked over open fire
  • ringing in the New Year camping out in the White Dessert
  • music of the Bedouin (argul (wind instrument) and tabla (drum))
  • sleeping under the stars (no tent - can't do that back home or you'd be eaten alive by mosquitoes) - only complaint is cold feet and face
  • driving back to Bhariyya; David and Mohammed Abdellatif in back with Mohammed on argul and David on tabla, driver singing and Kerala and Brenda in front clapping and "dancing"

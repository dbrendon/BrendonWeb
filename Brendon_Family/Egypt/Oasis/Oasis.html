Bhariyya Oasis

Bhariyya Oasis

December 30th - New Oasis Hotel

  • 5 hour bus ride to oasis from Cairo; mounds and mounds of garbage dumped by trucks or rail car by the side of the road, in the desert and/or railway track, movie played on the bus was "Water World", sand drifts on road; sun extremely bright and hot therefore curtains drawn
  • peacefulness, the green, trickling of water in the irrigation channels, steam coming off the hot spring water (Kerala playing with the warm water)
  • harvesting using donkey, Kerala had a ride
  • chickens, cows and goats roaming the streets
  • washouts of road resolved by throwing a few rocks where the truck tire could pass over
  • never-ending "hellos", "welcomes", "what your name", "where are you from"
  • doors to mud homes opening with families coming out just to say "hello" - we figure word got out somehow that we were walking through town - invitation for tea
  • moon-light dip in the hot spring

Easter Trip

Itinerary (~17 cities, 6800 km, in 18 days)

April 11-18

  • Day 1 [Denmark]: Leave Copenhagen, Friday night, 7:00 pm, ferry to 'old' East Germany, drive overnight to Munich.
  • Day 2 [Germany]: Spend the day in Munich; beer gardens. Drive to Salzburg in the evening.
  • Day 3 [Austria]: Tour area surrounding Salzburg. Scenic Road, Eagle's Nest. Salzburg old quarter.
  • Day 4 [Austria]: Salzburg Castle. Drive to Vienna after lunch. Tour Vienna downtown. Opera house.
  • Day 5 [Austria]: Vienna. Lipizzaner Stallions; Strauss concert; Kerala waltzes.
  • Day 6 [Austria]: Head for the Zillertal in the Alps (Finkenberg, Hintertux).
  • Day 7 [Austria]: Ski on a glacier, elevation 3600 m. (Hintertux).
  • Day 8 [Austria]: Stop in Innsbruck on our way to the 'Disney' castle in Füssen. Overnight in Leipheim.
  • Day 9 [Germany]: Black Forest scenic drive; Baden Baden baths; world's largest cuckoo clocks; Stag's Leap; overnight in Titisee.
  • Day 10 [Switzerland, Liechtenstein]: Look at Zurich, Switzerland on the way by; visit Vaduz, Liechtenstein; climb hill to Vaduz palace; try to get to the Matterhorn, but find out too late the the Oberalp Pass is closed; backtrack to spend night at Chur.
  • Day 11 [Switzerland]: Visit Interlaken region; place where Sherlock Holmes died; more mountain roads; Aigle; Montreux; Geneva.
  • Day 12 [France]: Drive to Paris; Boulevard Haussmann; Eiffel Tower from the gorund; Arc de Triomphe; Champs-Elysées.
  • Day 13 [France]: Notre Dame; Isle ???; Musée du Louvre; Obelisk; Bridge; Parks.
  • Day 14 [France]: La Défense; Grande Arche; Sacré Coeur; Moulin Rouge; climb the Eiffel Tower. Leave Paris at midnight toward North Holland.
  • Day 15 [Holland]: Alkamar cheese market; Tulips; Kuekonhof Gardens. drive to Roermond for work.
  • Day 16 [Holland]: Work.
  • Day 17 [Holland]: Work.
  • Day 18 [Germany]: Heading home. Stop to see historic Lübeck, Germany. Arrive 'home' at 8:00 pm.
